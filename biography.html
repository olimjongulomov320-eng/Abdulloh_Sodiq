Biography

Professional Profile

Abdulloh Sodiq is the Managing Partner of "Gulyamov, Sadikov & Partners" law firm and a licensed attorney with over 20 years of successful experience in law. He is one of the leading experts in business protection and legal support for major economic projects in Uzbekistan.

Through years of work in state law enforcement agencies and his deep analytical approach to working with business entities, he has earned a place among the country's most respected business lawyers.
